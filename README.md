# ProteinTracker

A super small, private-use iOS gadget for tracking your daily protein, water, and calorie intake.

## What it does

ProteinTracker lives on your home screen as a widget. At a glance it shows you where you're at for the day — protein in grams, calories in kcal, and water in ml.

Tap the widget to open the app. Inside you'll find a clean, simple interface with six buttons — one pair for each metric — to increase or decrease your values. That's it.

No history. No charts. No accounts. Just a straightforward day-to-day tracker that resets itself every morning.

## Features

- 🥩 Track daily protein (g)
- 🔥 Track daily calories (kcal)
- 💧 Track daily water (ml)
- 📲 Home screen widget — small or medium size
- 🔄 Auto-resets at midnight, every day
- 🔒 Fully private — no data ever leaves your device

## Built with

- Swift / SwiftUI
- WidgetKit
- UserDefaults (App Group)
